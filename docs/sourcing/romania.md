# Romania sourcing notes

## Best current target

- the national `rezidentiat` examination for medicine

## Why it matters

Romania has a large physician-training ecosystem and a clinically relevant national residency exam, so it is a strong candidate for a native benchmark track.

## Current blocker

The official ecosystem is visible through ministry and university channels, but we have not yet identified a stable, openly reusable archive of full question papers plus answer keys that can be integrated with high confidence and clear legal status.

## What would unlock integration

- an official archive of past exam question papers with answer keys
- a ministry or university release with explicit reuse terms
- a public extraction with clear provenance back to released official material

## Recommended next move

Keep Romania in the sourcing queue and prefer official exam archives or university-published releases over community mirrors or prep-site clones.
