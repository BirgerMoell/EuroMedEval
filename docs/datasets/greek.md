# Greek datasets

## Current seed dataset

- `doatap-med-el`: [Greek Medical MCQA](https://huggingface.co/datasets/ilsp/medical_mcqa_greek)

## Why it is included

The Greek seed set is a strong native benchmark anchor because it is:

- native-language
- drawn from real DOATAP medical exam material
- reconstructable from a public dataset card
- curated by ILSP / Athena Research Center

## Current EuroMedEval status

- task: `medical-knowledge-mcq`
- tier: `gold`
- status: `official`
- access mode: `script-only`

## Notes

The dataset card states that the questions were extracted from past exams published by the Hellenic National Academic Recognition and Information Center (DOATAP).
